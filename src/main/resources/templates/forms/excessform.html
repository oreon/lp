Notes to the Excess Liability insurance application form

You may print this instruction sheet and use it as a guide for completing the online application.
  1. For more information about the Excess Liability insurance coverage and options to increase your liability insurance protection, we recommend you download the Insurance Matters – Excess Liability Insurance booklet.
  2. Complete only one Application on behalf of the whole of the firm.
  3. Any Excess Liability insurance will be subject to a 60-day wait period following the date of receipt of the completed application form. Please note, if you are requesting coverage effective January 1, 2019, the application form must be received on or before December 1, 2018.
  4. Payment Options: You can provide your Premium Payment information electronically on this form, or by providing the relevant data by mail.
    • To authorize payment using a credit card: Complete question #14 and the authorization in question 16
    • To authorize payment using pre-authorized payments: Complete question #15 and the authorization in question 16
    • To mail in the required authorization forms, proceed to Premium Payment Authorization Form
  5. Complete the Warranty & Signature section with your name and Law Society of Ontario number, and the checkbox.
  6. Click on "Review" to see a preliminary copy of your completed application; if any of the questions have not been answered (or have been answered incorrectly), a message will appear with the appropriate direction.
  7. When you have completed your review and are ready to submit your filing, print a copy of your completed application for your records. Note that your application will not be considered received by LAWPRO until you press the "Submit" button and receive your confirmation number (beginning with "XS"). Record this confirmation number along with your printed application.
Please note: Your Excess premium invoice and Declarations page will be issued and available after your application is processed and the policy is instated. Please do not provide payment until after receiving your Excess premium invoice.
